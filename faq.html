Help Center

FAQs & Return Policy

Click the triangle to open each answer.

How can I customize my product?

Choose a product from the Products page, click Customize Now, select the color, upload your design, and add any special instructions.

What file types can I upload?

You can upload common image files such as JPG, PNG, JPEG, and JFIF.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery time depends on the product type, design details, and location. The customer support team will confirm the expected time after the order is reviewed.

Can I change my design after submitting the order?

You can request a design change before printing starts. After production begins, changes may not be possible.

Return Policy: Can customized products be returned?

Customized products cannot be returned unless there is a printing error, damaged item, or wrong product delivered.

Return Policy: How many days do I have to report a problem?

Please contact us within 3 days of receiving the order and include clear photos of the issue.

Return Policy: What happens if the mistake is from the store?

If the mistake is confirmed, we will offer a replacement or a suitable solution based on the order case.
